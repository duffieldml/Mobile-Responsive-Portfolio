About Me

I'm Matt Duffield, a 30 year old living in Grand Rapids, MI. I was born January 21st, 1991. I grew in Caledonia, Michigan. I attended Grand Valley State University for 2 years once I graduated high school in 2009. I was going after a photography degree, but I dropped out before I could complete my degree.

After dropping out of Grand Valley, I started working at Paragon Die & Engineering. I met my wife in 2016. After a couple months of dating, she finished her college degree for elementary teaching. She started looking for jobs all over the states to utilize her new degree. Miranda, my wife, was able to find a job in Phoenix, Arizona. I made the decision to leave Michigan behind and follow her.

At that point, I had been a Paragon Die & Engineering employee for 5 years. I spoke with the CEO of the company and put in my 2 week notice. The next day, the CEO came out and asked if I would like to continue working for Paragon. They decided to let me program CNC machines from home. It was a great opportunity for me.

I lived in Phoenix with my wife for 2 years before we moved back to Michigan. I fell in love with Arizona during my time there. The amount of outdoor activities available was fantastic. I loved hiking in the mountains around the city. Plus, not having to deal with snow was a huge plus. The worst part was the heat. Don't listen to people that say its a dry heat and its not that bad. Sure, it may be a dry heat, but 115 degrees is still hot.
